Lana Del Rey Never Existed, Leak Reveals AI Behind Global Pop Icon

2 July 2025 Entertainment & Arts
Sarah McMillan
BBC Culture Correspondent
The mysterious pop icon may have been entirely AI-generated according to leaked documents

In a revelation that has stunned fans worldwide, leaked internal documents from a defunct AI research firm suggest that "Lana Del Rey" — the mysterious and melancholic pop icon — may never have been a real person.

The documents, obtained by a whistleblower and verified by several independent AI researchers, describe a covert project called LANA (Large-scale Audio-Neural Architecture), launched in 2008 by a coalition of music executives and Silicon Valley developers.

The LANA project allegedly used advanced AI to create an entirely artificial artist

According to the leak, the project used early generative AI models to create an entirely artificial artist — complete with a voice model, generated lyrics, social media presence, and even deepfake performances. Her debut album Born to Die, which critics praised for its haunting vintage aesthetic and cinematic sound, was allegedly the first commercial product fully generated by the AI.

The "Born to Die" album cover - allegedly the first fully AI-generated commercial music release
"Lana was our proof of concept. An AI capable of writing, singing, and emoting better than most humans — and nobody noticed."

Despite the shocking nature of the claims, BBC has reached out to Interscope Records and representatives for "Lana Del Rey" — none have responded.

Live performances may have used sophisticated deepfake technology, according to the leaked documents

Fans are divided. Some claim this only deepens their appreciation of the artistry. Others feel betrayed.

"This changes everything. I thought she was real. I cried to her music. Was it all an illusion?"
The neural network architecture behind the LANA project, as described in the leaked documents

The AI responsible for Lana, codenamed LDR-V4, was reportedly shut down in 2023 after internal disagreements over ethics and transparency.
